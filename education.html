Middle School

The middle school that I attended from 2010 to 2019 is called "Blazhe Koneski" and is located in Prilep. I had some of the best moments there and got my very first friends for lifetime. I was an excellent student, which helped me enroll in high school.

High School

The high school that I attended from 2019 to 2023 is called Gimnazija "Mirche Acev", also located in Prilep. Sadly, due to the globally spread virus, I only went there for 2 years - physically and the other 2 years we had online classes. Once again, I was a straight A's student which opened the doors to a good university for me.

University

Currently, I attend the "Ss. Cyril and Methodius" University in Skopje, more precisely the faculty of information sciences and computer engineering. I am at the end of first year as a student, and I can say that it is everything i have expected from it, with a little surprises.
